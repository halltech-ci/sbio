Product Pack

Increase your sales with Product Pack in Odoo!

Odoo Product Pack allows you to cross-sell or upsell. You can bundle multiple items together to make product packs like beauty product packs, gym packs, etc. You can create and add a product pack to the order line, and the whole bundle will appear as one single product consisting of all the items.

Why do you Need Odoo Product Pack?

Odoo Product Pack allows you to sell products in packs. Also, it enables you to clear out slow-moving products from the store. Product Pack is a proven way for sellers to clear out their old and slow-moving products to add-up revenues.

The admin can offer great discounts on product packs to customers. And a single bundle contains multiple products that customers need, providing them with all things at once. The module also calculates the total price of all the items so you can decide the selling price of the pack.

Admin can choose whether to add tax on price or not. The order line will show the product pack name, price, and quantity. In addition, you can create a product pack, which has multiple product packs.

Advantages of Odoo Product Pack

  • It allows you to offer product bundles to customers.
  • Also, help in clearing out old products and slow-moving products in packs.
  • Enables customers to purchase all the products at once in pack form.
  • Product pack is a great marketing tool to earn profit and sales.
  • It helps retain old customers and keep them committed to your brand.
  • Also, product packs attract new customers and compel them to go for instant purchases.

Allows To Create Product Packs In Odoo

  • The module allows you to create product packs or bundles in Odoo.
  • The product pack can contain different products bundled together as one unit.

Can Combine More Than Two Products In A Pack

  • The module lets you combine two or more products to create a pack.
  • Just click the 'Add Pack' button, and your product will be added to the order line.

Create A Separate Menu For Creating Packs

  • It gives a separate menu for creating packs.
  • Once created, the pack will act similarly to other products.

Add The Product Pack In Sales Order

  • The admin can add the product pack to the order line in the sales order.
  • Choose to manage inventory at the product pack level or the individual product level.
