POS Product Pack

Product pack is a combination of several products put together at a special price. Product pack is created with a view to increase sales of the complementary products and to provide special offers to the customers to influence your sales. Now sellers using Odooâ€™s Point Of Sale can sell products in packs as a bundle product or in combinations using our module product pack for Point Of Sale. Seller can put multiple products consists of any number of units to a product pack at a lower price compared to buying them individually.

Salient Features
  • In Point Of Sale, seller can create a separate category for product packs.
  • Products packs can be identified easily on the home page.
  • Multiple products consist of any number of units can be put together in a product pack.
  • Invoice will have the information about the amount saved on purchase of a product packs.
  • POS Session View
  • Backend Configuration
